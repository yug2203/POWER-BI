# 📊 Indiclub Sales Analytics Dashboard

A comprehensive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze sales performance, product demand, marketplace contribution, and SKU mapping for **Indiclub**.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project provides an interactive sales analytics solution built in Power BI. The dashboard helps business stakeholders monitor sales trends, identify top-performing products, evaluate marketplace performance, and analyze regional demand patterns.

The dashboard is designed to support data-driven decision-making through intuitive visualizations and dynamic filtering capabilities.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall sales quantity performance
* Identify top-performing marketplaces
* Monitor design-wise product demand
* Track daily sales trends
* Understand state-wise sales distribution
* Perform Parent SKU ↔ Child SKU mapping analysis
* Support inventory and sales planning

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### Marketplace Performance Analysis

Analyze quantity sold across various marketplaces such as:

* AJIO_MODA
* F_PRIORITY
* F_STS
* MY_MODAMINGLE
* INDICLUB
* Snapdeal
* Priority Fashion

### Design-Wise Sales Analysis

Identify the best-performing product designs and their contribution to total sales.

Top Designs:

* PT27
* PT19
* PT22
* 2444
* SRTC
* SRTW

### Daily Sales Trend Analysis

Track quantity sold on a day-to-day basis to identify:

* Sales peaks
* Demand fluctuations
* Seasonal trends
* Business growth patterns

### State-Wise Sales Distribution

Visualize sales performance across different Indian states.

Top Performing States:

* Gujarat
* Maharashtra
* Uttar Pradesh
* Andhra Pradesh
* Karnataka

### SKU Mapping Analysis

Analyze relationships between:

* Parent Combo SKU
* Child SKU
* Quantity Sold

This helps improve inventory management and product tracking.
